About

This Music system combines a music player with an audio-based recommendation system, providing users with a personalized, and efficient way to enjoy music. The music player component of this project offers a range of features designed to enhance user experience such as repeat and lyric display.
The system conducts an analysis of audio features, including tempo and acoustic attributes, creating detailed profiles for each song. By learning from the user's listening history, the system understands their favorite moods, and song types. Combining audio profiles and user preferences, the system tailor's music recommendations to the individual user. Users receive song suggestions that align with their unique musical tastes.
The recommendation system introduces users to new songs, artists that resonate with their musical interests, fostering exploration and discovery. Users can add recommended songs to their playlists and play them instantly, making the process of enjoying new music seamless
